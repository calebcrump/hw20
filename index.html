Department of Energy Redesign

Users were frustrated with how long it takes them to locate important information on the Department of Energy website. My solution was a reorganization of the site layout, prioritizing information that is valuable to the user.

My Responsibilities, UX Researcher, UI Designer, Prototyping, & Testing.

Research

For user testing users were asked to complete one task: Find information and or resources on the energy.gov website regarding buying and driving fuel efficient and/or alternative fuel vehicles. 4 Users were interviewed, all were able to complete the task but some struggled and were displeased by the website layout. Users were asked what they liked and disliked about the sites layout and information. (In which they stressed how difficult it was to find the information they were looking for.) Users were also asked what they would use the energy.gov website for if they visited it.

Results
Ideation & Results

After conducting user research and testing, I came to the realization that most users were looking for information pertaining to solar panels and fuel efficient cars. This realization led to the restructuruing of the homepage.
